Privacy Policy – DreamSphere

Last updated: 2025.09.26

DreamSphere (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide while using our mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • User Input: Dream texts and notes that you voluntarily submit to the App.
  • Account Information: Email address or login credentials if you register an account.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, and basic analytics.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, such as screens viewed and actions taken.
  • Third-Party Data: Information collected by third-party services integrated into the App (e.g., advertising or payment providers).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-based dream interpretations and personalized features.
  • To maintain and improve the App’s performance and functionality.
  • To deliver personalized ads and measure ad effectiveness.
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • To ensure compliance with legal obligations and policies.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share limited information with trusted third parties:

  • Analytics and Advertising Providers (e.g., Google AdMob).
  • Payment Processors to handle in-app purchases.
  • Legal Authorities when required by applicable law.

Data Retention

Dream texts and user data are stored securely and retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Children’s Privacy

DreamSphere is not directed to children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover that a child has provided information, we will delete it immediately.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising.
  • Request data deletion by contacting us.

Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be published within the App and/or at the URL provided.
