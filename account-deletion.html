Account & Data Deletion

DreamSphere

If you would like to delete your DreamSphere account and associated data (including dream entries, profile information, and usage history), you have two options:

1) Request via Email

Send an email to: dnzdmrl91@gmail.com
We will process your request within 7 business days and permanently delete your data from our servers.

2) Request Inside the App

You can request deletion directly in the app under Profile → Delete Account.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account details (email and profile data)
  • Dream texts and related interpretations
  • Usage history linked to your account

What May Be Retained

Certain minimal records may be retained if required by applicable law (e.g., payment records), after which they will be securely deleted according to our retention policies.
